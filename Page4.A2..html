Ingredients
1 cup sticky rice 4 to 5 cups of Water 1 to 2 inch of Ginger 3 cloves of Garlic 1 chopped Onion 1 pounds of cutted into 4 piece Chicken 2 tablespoon of Patis 2 teaspoon of Salt and pepper for the taste 3 chopped of Green onion (optional) 2 cut of Hard boiled eggs (optional)
Guide How To Cook Longsilog
Pour water in a cooking pot. Bring to a boil. Put-in the rice. Continue cooking for 30 minutes or until the texture becomes thick, while stirring once in a while. Then add 4 piece of Chicken and ginger,garlic and onion then add salt and stir it and then cook for 2 minutes more and Top with a tablespoon of Green onions after that transfer it to your bowl and your done, you can serve it now
