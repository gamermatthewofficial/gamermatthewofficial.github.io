Mechado

Photo Reference Link
Ingridients
3 cloves garlic crushed 1 piece large onion sliced 2 lbs beef chuck cubed 8 ounces tomato sauce 1/2 cup water 3 tbsp cooking oil 1 slice lemon with rind 1 piece large potato sliced 1/4 cup soy sauce 1/2 tsp. ground black pepper 2 pieces bay leaves laurel 1 salt to taste
Guide How To Cook Mechado
first put the oil in the pan and wait until its hot after that put the onion and garlic put the beef in the pan wait till it turn brown then add the tomato sauce then add the water in 60 or 120 minutes then put the potato wait until it soften add the rest of the ingriendients after it cooked, put it in your bowl, youre done cooking
