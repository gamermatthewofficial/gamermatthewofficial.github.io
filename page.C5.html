Kare-Kare

Photo Reference Link
Ingredients
- 1 piece small banana flower bud, sliced - 1 bundle of pechay or bok choy - 1 bundle of string beans, cut into 2-inch slices - 4 pieces of eggplants, sliced - 1 cup ground peanuts - 1/2 cup peanut butter - 1/2 cup shrimp paste - 34 ounces of water (about 1 liter) - 1/2 cup annatto seeds, soaked in a cup of water - 1/2 cup toasted ground rice - 1 tablespoon of minced garlic - 1 piece of onion, chopped - Salt and pepper for the taste
Guide How To Cook Kare-Kare
Instructions: In a large pot, bring the water to a boil. Add the oxtail followed by the onions and simmer for 2.5 to 3 hours or until tender (or 35 minutes if using a pressure cooker). Once the meat is tender, add the ground peanuts, peanut butter, and coloring (water from the annatto seed mixture) and simmer for 5 to 7 minutes. Add the toasted ground rice and simmer for an additional 5 minutes. In a separate pan, saut√© the garlic, then add the banana flower, eggplant, and string beans, and cook for 5 minutes. Transfer the cooked vegetables to the large pot with the rest of the ingredients. Add salt and pepper to taste. after it pour it on your bowl and serve it
