Halo-Halo

Photo Reference Link
Ingredients
1 cup of Shaved ice 1 cup of Evaporated milk 5 banana slices 5 jackfruit slice 1 scoop of Leche flan 1 scoop of Ube 4 to 3 Beans 4 slice of Jelly (gulaman ) 4 macapuno 2 teaspoon of Sugar
Guide How To make Halo-Halo
In your tall plastic cup or tall glass cup, start with a layer of the sweetened beans at the bottom the add a Layer of the banana slices and jackfruit slices on top of the beans Next, add the gulaman slices and macapuno then Fill the glass with the shaved ice, packing it gently after it now Place the scoop of ube and leche flan on top of the shaved ice then Drizzle the evaporated milk over the entire mixture. After it sprinkle the sugar on top and mix everything together before eat, youre done
