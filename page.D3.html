Ube Halaya

Photo Reference Link
Ingredients
-2 cups grated ube (purple yam) or ube puree -1 cup coconut milk -1 cup evaporated milk -1 cup condensed milk -1/2 cup sugar for the taste -1/2 cup butter or margarine -1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional) -And a Pinch of salt
Guide How To make Ube halaya
If you are using fresh ube, peel, steam, and mash it until smooth. If using frozen grated ube or ube puree, thaw it first then In a large pan, combine the ube, coconut milk, evaporated milk, condensed milk, sugar, butter, and a pinch of salt. Mix well after it Cook over medium heat, stirring continuously to prevent sticking. Continue until the mixture thickens and starts to pull away from the sides of the pan (about 20-30 minutes). If you wanted to add vanila thenStir in the vanilla extract if using. After thatPour the mixture into a greased dish or mold, spreading it evenly. Smooth the top with a spatula thenAllow it to cool at room temperature, then refrigerate until set.
