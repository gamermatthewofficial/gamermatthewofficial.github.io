Leche flan

Ingredients
For the caramel on top -1 cup sugar -1/4 cup water For the flan: -6 large eggs -1 can (390 ml) condensed milk -1 can (370 ml) evaporated milk -1 teaspoon vanilla extract -A Pinch of salt
Guide How To make Ube halaya
To Make the Caramel, In a saucepan, combine sugar and water over medium heat. Stir until the sugar dissolves, then stop stirring. Allow it to simmer until it turns golden brown (about 5-10 minutes) then Carefully pour the caramel into a llanera (flan mold) or a round baking dish, tilting to coat the bottom evenly. Set aside to cool. Now Prepare the Flan Mixture, In a mixing bowl, whisk the eggs gently. Add the condensed milk, evaporated milk, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Mix until well combined, but avoid creating too many bubbles then Strain the mixture through a fine sieve into the prepared mold to remove any lumps after it you will bake it you need to Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Place the flan mold in a larger baking dish and fill the outer dish with hot water to create a water bath you need to Bake it for about 50-60 minutes, or until set (a knife inserted should come out clean) then Remove from the oven and allow to cool at room temperature. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight,Youre Done
