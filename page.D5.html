Turon

Photo Reference Link 3
Ingredients
-4 ripe saba bananas -1 cup ripe jackfruit -1 cup brown sugar -8 spring roll wrappers -1 cup of Oil for frying
Guide How To make Turon
first Prepare the Bananas Peel the saba bananas and slice them in half lengthwise. Then Add Jackfruit and cut it into strips then Place the brown sugar on a plate. Roll each banana (and jackfruit slice, if using) in the sugar until well coated after that Lay a spring roll wrapper on a flat surface, place a banana (and jackfruit) near the bottom edge. Fold the sides over the filling and roll it up tightly. Seal the edges with a bit of water then Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Once hot, carefully add the wrapped turon. Fry until golden brown and crispy, turning occasionally (about 4-5 minutes) For the extra sweetness, add a bit of the leftover brown sugar to the oil while frying, allowing it to caramelize and coat the turon then Remove from the oil and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
