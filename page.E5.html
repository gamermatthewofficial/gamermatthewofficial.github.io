Sago't Gulaman

Ingredients
For the Sago pearls -1 cup sago pearls For the Gulaman -1 package (1/2 oz) gelatin powder -2 cups water (for dissolving gelatin) -1 cup sugar (adjust to taste) -1-2 cups water (for the syrup) For the Drink -4 cups water (for the drink) -1/2 cup brown sugar (or to taste) -1 kilo Ice cubes
Guide How To make guyabano juice
First Prepare the Sago pearls you need to Boil water in a pot. Add the sago pearls and cook according to package instructions until they become translucent (about 15-20 minutes). Stir occasionally to prevent sticking Once cooked, drain and rinse with cold water to stop the cooking process. Set aside. Now you have to Make the Gulaman First In a separate pot, dissolve the gelatin powder in 2 cups of water. Add sugar and stir well. Heat over medium heat until it boils, then simmer for about 5 minutes until fully dissolved then Pour the mixture into a mold or shallow dish and let it cool until set (about 30 minutes to 1 hour). Once set, cut into cubes after that you need to Prepare the Drink In a separate pot, combine 4 cups of water and brown sugar. Heat until the sugar dissolves completely. Allow to cool. Lastly you need In a glass cup, combine a few tablespoons of sago, gulaman cubes, and pour in the sweetened water. Add ice cubes then stir it, Youre Done.
