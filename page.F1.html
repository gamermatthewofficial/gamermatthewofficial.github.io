Kutsinta

Photo Reference Link
Ingredients
-1 cup rice flour -1/2 cup brown sugar -1/2 cup water -1/2 teaspoon lye water ( for color and texture) -1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract -Grated coconut
Guide How To Cook kutsinta
In a mixing bowl, combine rice flour and brown sugar. Gradually add water and lye water (if using), mixing until smooth. You can add vanilla extract for extra flavor then Fill a steamer pot with water and bring it to a boil. Grease small molds or cupcake liners to prevent sticking after that Pour the kutsinta batter into the greased molds, filling them about 3/4 full then Place the molds in the steamer and cover. Steam for about 30-40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean Lastly Remove the molds from the steamer and allow them to cool completely before unmolding
