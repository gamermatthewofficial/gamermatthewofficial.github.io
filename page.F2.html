Puto

Photo Reference Link
Ingredients
-2 cups rice flour -1 cup sugar -1 tablespoon baking powder -1/2 teaspoon salt -11/2 cups coconut milk (or water) -1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional) -Cheese slices or salted egg (for topping)
Guide How To Cook puto
First you need to Prepare the Mixture In a mixing bowl, combine rice flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Mix well Gradually add coconut milk and vanilla extract (if using), stirring until smooth then Fill a steamer pot with water and bring it to a boil. Grease small molds or cupcake liners after that Pour the puto batter into the greased molds, filling them about 3/4 full. If using, place a slice of cheese or salted egg on top of each then Place the molds in the steamer and cover. Steam for about 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean Lastly Remove the molds from the steamer and let them cool slightly before unmolding, Youre Done
