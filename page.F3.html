Ingredients
-6 ripe saba bananas (or any firm banana) -1 cup brown sugar -Oil for frying
Guide How To Cook banana cue
First Peel the saba bananas and leave them whole or cut them in half, depending on your preference then Place the brown sugar on a plate. Roll each banana in the sugar until well coated then In a frying pan, heat enough oil over medium heat to cover the bottom of the pan after that Add the sugar-coated bananas to the hot oil. Fry until they turn golden brown and the sugar caramelizes, turning them occasionally (about 5-7 minutes) after that Remove the bananas from the pan and let them drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
