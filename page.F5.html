Ingredients
-12 quail eggs -1 cup all-purpose flour -1/2 cup cornstarch -1 teaspoon baking powder -1/2 teaspoon salt -1 cup water (adjust as needed) -Orange food coloring
Guide How To Cook kwek kwek
First Boil the quail eggs in water for about 5-7 minutes. Once cooked, cool them under cold running water, then peel the shells then in a mixing bowl, combine flour, cornstarch, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add water, mixing until smooth. Add orange food coloring to achieve the desired color after that In a deep pan or pot, heat enough oil for deep frying over medium heat then Dip each peeled quail egg into the batter, ensuring it's fully coated and now Carefully drop the battered eggs into the hot oil, frying in batches until golden brown (about 3-5 minutes). Avoid overcrowding the pan lastly Remove the kwek-kwek from the oil and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil, After it put it on your plate,Youre Done
