Tinola

Ingredients
1 lb chicken 1 onion, sliced 4 cloves garlic, minced 2 inches ginger, sliced 1-2 tablespoon patis 4 cups water 1 Sayote peeled and sliced 1-2 cups malunggay leaves Salt and pepper for the Taste
Guide How To Cook Tinola
In the large pot, put a bit of oil over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, and ginger, and saut√© until fragrant add the chicken pieces and cook until they are lightly browned Pour in the patis and stir for a minute to enhance the flavor then Pour in the water or chicken broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for about 20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through then. Add the sayote and simmer for another 10-15 minutes, until the vegetables are tender, then stir in the spinach or malunggay leaves. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and let it cook for another 2-3 minutes after You're done cooking
