Longsilog

Photo Reference Link
Ingredients
1/2 lb. skinless longanisa 1 pieces eggs 4 cups of fried rice 5 cloves garlic crushed 1/2 cup water 4 tablespoons cooking oil For the Taste add salt and pepper
Guide How To Cook Longsilog
First Boil the longganisa with enough water then Continue to boil until the water evaporates from the pan then fry the longganisa Until it is cooked. After that, you need to cook a sunny side-up egg, and put it in your fried rice On the Plate, and then you're Done.
